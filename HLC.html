About AOFLAH HLC

  • HLC is currently running to its 12 years from humble beginning as chamber choir, and despite the countless challenges and trial this organization encounters. They delivered the duties and fullfil their purpose entrusted to them by the help of our Lord God.

  • HLC performers/members are composed by 3 kapisanans. Buklod, Kadiwa and Binhi. Regardless the diversity, we've something in common.The love in music and the goal to praise the Almighty God.

Mission

Share the vibe, commitment and the love in music, with the highest regard to sing praises to our Lord God whole heartedly, mind and soul.

Vision

To inspire, foster and transform the choral music sharing the love in music across generations.

Commitment

  • To deliver the enstruted duties
  • To grow and hone skills in singing
  • To be role model
  • To uplift each member
  • To guide the new generation
